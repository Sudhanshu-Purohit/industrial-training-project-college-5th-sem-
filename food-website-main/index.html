Red Velvet Cake

Red velvet cake is a classic dessert that is known for its moist texture and rich cream cheese frosting. The cake itself is made with a combination of flour, sugar, eggs, and cocoa powder, with the addition of red food coloring, which gives it its distinctive red color. The frosting is typically made with cream cheese, butter, and powdered sugar, and is spread over the top and sides of the cake.

Red velvet cakes are usually enjoyed as a dessert, and can be consumed as a single layer or multi-layer cake. It's a popular choice for special occasions such as birthdays and weddings, but can also be enjoyed as an everyday treat. The cake's combination of sweetness and tanginess, along with the cream cheese frosting, makes it a favorite among many.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies are a classic treat that is loved by people of all ages. These cookies are made with flour, sugar, butter, eggs, and chocolate chips, which give them their delicious chocolate flavor. The ingredients are usually mixed together to form a dough and then shaped into small balls before baking. Chocolate chip cookies are often considered a comfort food and are a popular choice for a sweet snack or dessert.

Today, chocolate chip cookies are a staple in bakeries and homes around the world and come in many variations, such as gluten-free, vegan, and with different types of chocolate.

What do i get my refund?

If the customer had an issue with the product and it is still under the warranty period, the bakery will either replace it or give the customer a refund after evaluating the issue.

How long it will take food to arrive?

The time it takes for food to arrive will depend on a number of factors such as the location of the bakery, the location of the delivery destination, the method of delivery, and the current volume of orders.

What are the options for gluten-free products at your bakery?

our bakery offers a variety of gluten-free options such as breads, pastries, cakes, and cookies.

What is cost of food delivery?

Our bakery offers delivery within a 5-mile radius for a flat fee of RS 40. Orders over RS 300 are eligible for free delivery. For deliveries beyond the 5-mile radius, we charge an additional RS 10 per mile.

Who is eligible for pro membership?

Our bakery offers a pro membership program for our regular customers. To be eligible for pro membership, customers must make at least 10 purchases in a calendar year and spend a minimum of RS 5000.
